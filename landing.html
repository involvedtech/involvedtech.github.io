Our Approach

All Parents on One Platform

We have pioneered an approach in line with GDPR that ensures all parents are encouraged to get involved and engage with your school. Our support team is on hand to guide parents through the simple set-up process. Access to parents through a single platform is vital for improving efficiency in schools.

Provide Meaningful Information

Our innovations are always backed by research. Our research told us that for parents to take up an active role in their child’s education, they want information that is regular, meaningful and specific to their child’s attainment. And so, we have teamed up with SIMS to provide parents just what they need.

Empower Teachers

To empower parents, it is important to empower teachers first. It is for this reason that we provide teachers with the ability to communicate directly with a parent in an instant. To truly boost attainment and well-being of students, teachers must be empowered with the relevant skills in school to home communication.

Parents & Students

Empowered to take action

Access via a Single App

Our app is the home for key information parents and students need on a daily basis to get involved. Parents with more than one child can track progress, attendance, view timetable and homework of all their children using a single account.

Manage School Homework

Parents and Students clearly view all the homework, projects and class test as set by teachers. Our apps encourage students to request support where necessary and organise tasks by due date to promote timely homework submissions.

Push Notifications

Parents and Students receive automated notifications straight into the palms of their hand when it comes to new homework and communication from teachers. Push notifications are brilliant at engaging parents and students in school work.

View Live Reports

Our live reports and interactive charts are fantastic for parents and students to review their ongoing performance and take action. No longer do parents and students have to search for old progress reports to track their previous grades.

Safe, Secure and Private

Parents, Student and Teachers can use our platform with their minds at ease. We go the extra mile to protect your data. Our robust processes ensure that data is kept secure, and only visible to the responsible user.

Teachers

Share, engage and collaborate

Instant Access to Parents

Direct communication between teachers and parents is vital to boost student attainment and well-being. Using InvolvEd’s Messaging tool, teachers can discuss opportunities, share responsibility and reach out to parents in an instant with the help of push notifications.

Efficient, yet Functional

It’s simple, we want teachers to spend as little time as possible on InvolvEd but their actions to have a significant impact. So, our teacher’s web app has been designed with an intuitive interface that makes setting homework a breeze.

A Focus on Teaching

We understand the importance for teachers to stay on top of their work. To save time, we have automated a daily reminder email that will notify teachers of any homework due for collection and/or any unread messages from parents.

Access to Attainment Data

With InvolvEd, teachers can view and compare a student’s attainment information across all his/her subjects. This information is crucial for teachers to identify and share a student’s learning and development needs with their parents.
