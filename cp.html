Cookies Policy

Welcome to InvolvEd - thank you for using our products and services to improve and simplify parental engagement.

InvolvEdTech LLP of 27 Old Gloucester Street, London, WC1N 3AX uses cookies to provide the services of their InvolvEd web application. The cookies can be split into three categories. Cookies that are required to provide our services to you. Cookies that enhance the performance of our services and cookies that assist the functionality of our services.

Some of these cookies are persistent cookies that remain with your machine upon ceasing use of the InvolvEd application. Other cookies are session cookies that expire upon ceasing the current session of use for our services.

Required

The required cookies are necessary to provide the InvolvEd services to you. These cookies allow us to ensure that you are a verified and authorised user of InvolvEd, in turn protecting your personal data. Without these cookies being stored on your device, unfortunately we would not be able to provide our services to you.

Enhance Performance

Cookies that enhance performance are designed to provide an efficient service to you. Some of our services request data from a database. In order to save loading time and ensure that you receive information at an instant we use these cookies. Without these cookies being stored on your device, there may be some lag in screens loading or some abnormalities on screens.

Assist Functionality

Cookies that assist functionality are designed to provide a great user experience when using our services. They help with functionality such as: remember my e-mail and password, remembering the week you were viewing when addressing tasks, etc. These cookies will make the web application more responsive, efficient and easy to use.

You may choose to refuse cookies using your browser settings. This may partially or completely impact on your ability to use our services. If your browser settings are not configured to refuse cookies, then cookies will automatically be issued upon login to our services.

For more information about cookies, please visit http://www.aboutcookies.org/ or contact us on support@involvedtech.co.uk.
